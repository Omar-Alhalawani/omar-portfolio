About

Hello there

I'm Omar — a curious problem-solver building embedded, FPGA, and robotics systems

I’m a Computer Systems Engineering student at Carleton University with a focus on embedded systems, FPGA/digital logic, robotics, and Python-based tooling. I enjoy projects where hardware and software meet and where the results are visible: motors moving, signals updating, data streaming.

My experience includes developing a Mini-Lysimeter Research GUI (NI-DAQ + Arduino), creating EMG data acquisition tools for BioCARE, building FPGA logic modules, and repairing electronics through my own repair initiative. I like engineering work that is hands-on, useful, and grounded in real-world needs.
